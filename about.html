Our Story

About Mwezi

Where clinical excellence meets deep rest, renewal and transformation in the heart of Zambia.

Who We Are

Holistic Healing,
Rooted in Evidence

Mwezi Wellness & Retreat is a holistic mental health and wellness center offering evidence-based clinical care integrated with psychotherapy, nutrition-based mental health, hydrotherapy, and immersive retreat experiences.

The name Mwezi means "moon" in Swahili — a symbol of cycles, renewal, and the quiet power of reflection. Like the moon, we believe healing comes in phases: recognising what needs to change, resetting your foundation, and restarting with clarity and purpose.

We serve individuals, families, professionals, and organisations seeking sustainable healing, emotional resilience, and mental clarity in a safe, confidential, and restorative environment.

"The main thing to a healthy life is having a healthy Mind, Body and Spirit."

Our Promise

At Mwezi Wellness & Retreat, healing is intentional, dignified and holistic. We provide a safe space where clinical excellence meets rest, reflection and renewal.

  • Supporting individuals to heal deeply
  • Helping families live well together
  • Empowering professionals to thrive sustainably
  • Partnering with organisations for lasting resilience
  • Confidential, safe, and evidence-based at every step

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Safety & Confidentiality

Every session, every conversation, and every record is held with the highest standard of professional confidentiality.

Evidence-Based Care

Our clinical interventions are grounded in research, delivered by qualified professionals trained in contemporary approaches.

Cultural Sensitivity

We honour African contexts, values, and lived experiences in all our therapeutic and wellness work.

Dignity & Respect

Every person who walks through our doors is treated with unconditional dignity, warmth and professional respect.

Holistic Approach

We treat the whole person — mind, body and spirit — recognising that true wellbeing cannot be separated into parts.

Sustainable Healing

We focus on lasting transformation, not quick fixes — building resilience, clarity and tools for life beyond therapy.
