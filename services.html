Our Services

Comprehensive care for mind, body and spirit — from clinical therapy to immersive Zambian retreat experiences.

Where Clinical Care Meets Deep Rest

What We Offer

Every service at Mwezi is delivered with dignity, clinical rigour, and a deep commitment to your lasting wellbeing.

01

Clinical Mental Health Services

Comprehensive, evidence-based mental health assessments and interventions delivered by qualified clinicians. We diagnose, treat, and support a full range of mental health conditions in a safe, confidential setting.

02

Psychotherapy & Talk Therapy

Individual therapy using CBT, trauma-informed, and person-centered approaches. We provide a non-judgmental space to explore emotions, patterns, and pathways toward healing and self-understanding.

03

Retreat-Based Healing Programs

Multi-day immersive programs combining therapy, nature, structured activities, and deep rest. Designed for those who need to step away from daily stress and invest fully in their healing.

04

Women's & Men's Wellness Programs

Gender-specific programs that address unique mental health challenges with community, peer support, and therapeutic facilitation. A space to be seen, heard, and supported by others who understand.

05

Adolescent & Young Adult Services

Specialised support for youth navigating identity, anxiety, academic pressure, relationships, and life transitions. We meet young people where they are and walk alongside them.

06

Group Programs & Support Circles

Facilitated peer support and therapeutic group sessions for shared healing and community resilience. Connection is medicine — our groups create belonging and collective strength.

07

Psychoeducation Workshops

Monthly and quarterly workshops that empower communities with mental health knowledge, coping tools, and awareness. Available for schools, workplaces, churches, and community groups.

08

Therapy + Safari Retreat Packages

Our most unique offering — combine transformative therapy sessions with the awe-inspiring experience of Zambia's wilderness. Healing through nature, stillness, and the power of Africa's wild.

Mwezi Signature Experience
09

Corporate Wellness Services

Tailored programs for organisations — employee wellness days, burnout prevention workshops, leadership mental health training, and resilience programs. Healthy teams build stronger organisations.

10

Nutrition-Based Mental Health Services

The gut-brain connection is real. We offer personalised nutrition plans that support emotional regulation, energy, sleep, and cognitive clarity as part of a holistic mental health approach.

11

Hydrotherapy Wellness Services

Therapeutic bath experiences using premium bath salts, bath bombs, and essential oils. Hydrotherapy activates the parasympathetic nervous system — promoting deep relaxation, stress relief, and better sleep.
